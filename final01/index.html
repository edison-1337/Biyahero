Our Services

Route Planning

The Route Planning feature enables commuters to identify the most efficient route to reach their destination at ICCT Colleges Cainta. It provides route options that help users select the most convenient and time-efficient path for their travel.

Fare Computation

The Fare Computation feature calculates the estimated fare required for a selected route. It also provides information about the standard fare rates and applicable discounts for students, senior citizens, and other eligible commuters when using public transportation.

Transfer Suggestion

The Transfer Suggestion feature recommends possible transfer points along the selected route. This allows commuters to identify the most optimal way to reach their destination while minimizing confusion during their trip.

About the System

The ICCT Jeepney Route Planner is a web-based platform designed to help students and commuters easily navigate jeepney routes going to and from ICCT Colleges Cainta Campus.

The system allows users to select their starting point, destination, and view the appropriate jeepney routes along with fare information.

This platform was developed as part of an academic project to improve transportation accessibility and commuter experience for ICCT students.
